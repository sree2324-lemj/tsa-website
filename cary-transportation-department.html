Cary Transportation Department

The Town of Cary's Transportation Department is responsible for planning, designing, and constructing all the transportation infrastructure across the city. This includes streets, sidewalks, and greenways. The department also operates the traffic management center, which is located at 316 North Academy Street. The department works closely with residents and businesses to help keep everyone safe.

The department operates GoCary, which provides public transportation options throughout Cary and the Triangle region as a whole. As a Bike Friendly community, Cary maintains a network of over 200 miles of greenways on bike facilities. Through all of this, the Transportation Department plays a vital role in keeping Cary connected and sustainable.
