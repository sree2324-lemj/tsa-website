Cary Arts Center

Cary Arts Center is the hub of arts activities in Cary. Its legacy of education began in the 1870s. That’s when Allison Frances Page, the founder of Cary, opened Cary Academy. The Cary Arts Center features a 431-seat theater. It also offers programs like the Marvelous Music Series and Applause! Cary Youth Theatre.

There are many things you can learn at the Cary Arts Center, including visual and performing arts. You can learn these activities in many ways. You might take classes or camps at the Cary Arts Center. You could also join a program like the Marvelous Music Series. There are even various studio arts that you could learn, such as woodworking, ceramics, or even textiles.
