Wake County Public School System

Wake County Public School System, or WCPSS is a public school district located in Cary. It was created in 1976 when the Raleigh City and Wake County School Systems came together to form one district. It is currently the largest School District in NC with over 161,000 students and more than 200 schools. The School System includes a mix of kids from elementary, middle, and high schools with various magnet programs and options that give people a choice in their education.

WCPSS focus of giving quality education to students from many different backgrounds and ethnicities. They offer many specialized programs, including STEM, Leadership, and arts magnet schools that support many different career pathways. The district is overseen by the Wake County Board of Education who work together to set policy, manage operations, and make sure educational standards are met. In Cary, WCPSS allows families to have access to many diffrent schools, which is vital to local education and youth develpment.
