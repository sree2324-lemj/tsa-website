Lake Crabtree County Park

Lake Crabtree County Park is a popular outdoor park located at 1400 Aviation Parkway, nestled between Cary, Morrisville, and the Raleigh-Durham International Airport. As the first established county park in Wake County, it features more than 16 miles of trails and sits on a 215-acre site. The park is home to a WaterWise Garden maintained by the Master Gardeners of Wake County.

The park offers many different activities, including hiking, mountain biking, seasonal boat rentals, two playgrounds, and many more. Visitors can rent sailboats, kayaks, rowboats, and paddleboats directly from the park's waterfront during boat rental season. The park also holds historical significance as the remains of a 19th-century tenant farm can be found there.
