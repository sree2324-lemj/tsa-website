Cary Players Community Center

Cary Players Community Theatre Company is a local community theatre organization located in Cary. They produce live shows for the public, which can include musicals, plays, and much more. They were founded in 2001 and have been driven by volunteer actors, directors, and crew. Performances are presented in places like the Page-Walker Arts and History Center and the Cary Arts Center.

Cary Players is a nonprofit organization that relies on volunteers to create shows, perform, and run the company. Auditions are open to everyone in the community, allowing people of all ages and experience levels the chance to do what they love. The theatre also offers affordable entertainment and helps strengthen the art scene and cultural life in Cary.
