Cary Arts Center

Cary Arts Center is the hub of arts activites in cary. its legacy of education started in the 1870's when the Town of Cary founder, Allison Frances Page, opened the Cary Academy. The Cary Arts Center not only has access to a 431-seat theatre, but it also has many programs supporting it, including Marvelous Music Series and Applause! Cary Youth Theatre.

There are many things you can learn in the Cary Arts Center, Including visual and performing arts. There are also many diffrent ways you can learn these activities, such as taking classes or camps at Cary Arts Center or joining a program like the Marvelous Music Series. There are even various studio arts that you could learn such as, woodworking, ceramics or even textiles.
