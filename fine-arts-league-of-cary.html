Fine Arts League Of Cary

The Fine Arts League of Cary is a nonprofit visual arts organization that has been serving the Triangle region of NC since 1996. Its main mission is to support the development of local artists while building greater public awareness of visual arts in the community. FALC was founded by a group of dedicated art enthusiasts, and because of that, it has grown into one of the largest recognized art organizations in North Carolina. With a membership open to everyone from beginners to art enthusiasts, FALC creates a welcoming space for anyone who has a passion for visual arts.

FALC connects many artists and art lovers through many different exhibitions, educational programs, and community events. The organization hosts different opportunities including juried art shows, youth art awards, and public art gatherings throughout North Carolina. These events allow both emerging and established artists to showcase their work and expand their palette. Its gallery is located at 110 E. Park Street in downtown Cary, serving as a creative hub where locals can experience great art up close. As the program continues to expand, FALC remains committed to enriching the next generation of artists in the Triangle region.
