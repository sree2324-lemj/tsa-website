Fine Arts League Of Cary

The fine arts league of cary is a non profit visual arts organization that has been serving the triangle region of NC since 1996. Its main mission is to support the development of local artists while building a greater public awareness of visual arts in the community. FALC was founded by a group of dedicated art enthusiants, and because of that it has grown into one of the largest recognized art organizations in North Carolina. With a membership open to everyone from beginers to art enthusiants, FALC creates a welcoming space for anyone who has a passion for visual arts.

FALC connects many artists and art lovers through many different exhibitions, educational programs, and community events. The organization hosts different opportunits including juried art shows, youth art awards, and public art gatherings througout North Carolina. These events allow both emerging and esthablished artists to showcase there work and expand there pallet. Its gallery is located at 110E. Park Street in downtown Cary serving as a creative hub where locals can experiance great art up close. As the program continues to expand, FALC remains commited to enriching the next generation of artists in the triangle region.
