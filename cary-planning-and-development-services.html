Cary Planning and Development Services

The Town of Cary's Planning and Development Services Department is responsible for planning the future growth of the community and, through careful land use, transportation planning, and affordable housing initiatives, the department is planning to make everyone's lives better. The department is organized into four main categories: administration, planning, community development, zoning administration, and development review and compliance. With all of these different categories, the Town of Cary's Planning and Development Services Department makes all of our lives better.

The Department is responsible for creating demographic data, maintaining and enforcing zoning, processing sign permits, and preparing maps. They also review all private development projects to ensure they meet the town's standards. Through all of these efforts, the department plays an important role in our lives and makes Cary one of the best places to live in .
