Cary Rotary Club

The Cary Rotary Club, founded in 1964, is a service organization that operates under the principle "Service Above Self." As a part of Rotary's International District 7710, the club has spent over 5 decades giving back to both local and international communities. With more than 100 people, the club brings together like-minded individuals that want to change the world for the better. Members meet regularly and discuss and plan different services to do to essentially benefit cary.

The Cary Rotary Club engages in a wide variety of volunteer efforts and community initiatives throughout the year. Locally, members participate in activities such as food drives, tutoring, yard cleanup, tree planting, diaper collection, and more. On a global scale, the club has also supported projects in Kenya, the Dominican Republic, and Honduras, focusing on education and clean water. Beyond service, the club also has a strong sense of fellowship from its members through dinners and community events.
