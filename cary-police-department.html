Cary Police Department

The Cary Police Department is the main law enforcement agency in Cary. Its mission is to partner with the community to provide excellent policing to ensure that Cary remains one of the safest cities out there. The Cary Police Department values excellence, teamwork, professionalism, ethical behavior, loyalty, and citizens.

Cary Police Department operates under a law enforcement philosophy called Geo-Policing, which allows officers to know exactly what needs, issues, and concerns people have in Cary. The Cary Police Department also offers programs like the Citizen Police Academy, which teaches people about Police operations through classroom instruction, Simulation, and Ride-alongs.
