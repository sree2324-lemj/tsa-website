Triangle Family Services

Triangle Family Services, also known as TFS, is a non-profit organization that serves families in the Triangle region of NC. Since 1937, its mission has been to build a stronger community by strengthening the family. Located at 3937 Western Blvd in Raleigh, TFS operates through 3 core beliefs: family safety, financial stability, and mental health. With nearly 90 years of service, the organization is very trustworthy and helps families survive.

TFS serves over 13,000 local residents every single year. They have many different family safety programs, including domestic violence intervention, supervised visitation, and street outreach for homeless people. These programs essentially help people in the Triangle region stay stable and not be dependent on others for the rest of their lives.
