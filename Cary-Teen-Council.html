Cary Teen Council

Cary Teen Council is a volenteering organization made up of young dedicated service minded teens from across the city. These teens are passionate about making a diffrence in this community by volenteering helping out Kids that cant help themselves, Serving food to the homeless and much much more. At Cary Teen council the young leaders are shaping the future of of this city.

Cary Teen Council has many volenteer opportunities from Serving food to the homeless all the way to helping out at a diaper bank. Their mission is to build leadership, accountability and a passion for service. They accomplish this through meaningful engagment by helping out around the city, They even have a council where they do Quartery council meanings. Cary Teen Council empowers teens to grow and inspire positive change.
