Cary Teen Council

Cary Teen Council is a volunteering organization made up of young dedicated service-minded teens from across the city. These teens are passionate about making a difference in this community by volunteering, helping out kids that can't help themselves, serving food to the homeless, and much, much more. At Cary Teen Council, the young leaders are shaping the future of this city.

Cary Teen Council has many volunteer opportunities, from serving food to the homeless all the way to helping out at a diaper bank. Their mission is to build leadership, accountability, and a passion for service. They do this through meaningful engagement by helping out around the city. They even have a council where they hold quarterly council meetings. Cary Teen Council empowers teens to grow and inspire positive change.
