Cary Visual Arts

Cary Visual Arts or CVA is a nonprofit organization committed to promoting, inspiring, and supporting visual arts throughout North Carolina. They believe that visual art enriches the community and everyday life. CVA works to bring high-quality art into the spaces where people live, work, and gather. Cary Visual Arts heavily relies on partnerships to keep it afloat, leaning on individuals, community groups, and local businesses to continue to spread visual art throughout the Triangle region of North Carolina. Through this collaborative approach, CVA has built a strong, lasting impression on the people of North Carolina.

Over the years, CVA has placed more than 50 works of art, valued at over a million dollars, at parks, businesses, and public properties across the Town of Cary. However, CVA also engages the community through sculpture art and lectures that teach people about the importance of art in our communities. The organization even has its own app called Otocast, which is a GPS-based audio tour app, allowing people to explore Cary's vast art selections easily.
