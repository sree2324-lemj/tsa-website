Food Bank of Central Eastern North Carolina

The Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina is a food bank that collects and distributes food through partner agencies. This ensures that the collected food can reach people that need it most. The Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina is one of the largest hunger relief organizaitons in the united states.

The Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina works to fight hunger by giving out food to famlies of low income or a lack of resources. They serve a network of more then 700 partner agencies inluding food pantrys, meal sites, and markets. The Food bank strives for a future where nobody in Eastern and Central North Carolina will suffer from hunger again.
