Cary Academy

Cary Academy or "CA" is a private college-prep school located in Cary. It serves middle and high school students and teaches them not only core subjects but also critical thinking and collaboration. Cary Academy has a very strong emphasis on getting students ready for college and making sure they succeed. One way they do this is by having smaller class sizes to give students more individualized attention.

Cary Academy is a very challenging school as it offers a wide range of AP and honors courses. It also has great STEM and humanities programs. Cary Academy is a very academic school as it integrates technology and project-based learning into its classes, but it also encourages students to join clubs, sports, and even various service projects.
