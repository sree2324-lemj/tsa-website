Applause! Cary Youth Theatre

Applause! Cary Youth Theatre is a youth performing arts program run by the Town of Cary that offers theatre and arts classes for those aged 4 to 14. The program has many theatre professionals and is dedicated to helping children build drama skills while encouraging confidence and creativity. Based at the Cary Arts Center at 101 Dry Avenue, Applause! provides a supportive environment to help children grow and learn many dance and theatre skills. The program's belief is that skills learned through theatre help prepare students for success in life.

Applause! connects its students to many different productions and public performances throughout the year. Teens between the ages of 13 and 18 can audition for full productions and have the chance to perform for other people live, and younger students can participate in classes and camp showcases to still show off their theater skills. Applause! remains committed to making theater accessible to everyone and helping more people find joy in the performing arts.
