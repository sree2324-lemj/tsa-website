Spruce Program

The Spruce program is a volunteer organization run by the Town of Cary. It was founded in 2009 with the goal of keeping Cary "Clean and Forever Green." The program connects many different groups and puts them through hands-on environmental service to benefit the community. Since it was founded, the Spruce program has hosted 1,895 projects with the support of more than 25,866 volunteers, making it one of the most successful environmental efforts in history.

Spruce has many different ways that people can benefit the environment. One of its most popular is the Adopt a Spot program, where people commit to keeping a certain area clean and litter-free. The program also organizes landscaping events, including tree planting and compost giveaways.
