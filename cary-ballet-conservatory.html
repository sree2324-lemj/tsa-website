Cary Ballet Conservatory

The Cary Ballet Conservatory is a very popular dance school located at 920 High House Rd in Cary, North Carolina. Founded in August 1995, the school has nearly 3 decades of experience teaching young dancers, and because of that, it has become one of the most respected ballet schools in the entire state. The conservatory's guiding motto, "dance skills are life skills," shows that they truly value dance and ballet as a whole and believe it helps prepare students for success in all areas of life. The conservatory's faculty is made up of highly accomplished professionals who are dedicated to making their students the best dancers that they can be.

The Cary ballet conservatory offers a wide range of programs made for dancers of all skill levels to improve their technique, from pre-ballet and recreational classes to a rigorous and professional Training program. Students are pushed to the limit so they can eventually become successful. Students in the professional divison have already gone to perform with world-renowed companys like New york city ballet and Miami city ballet. The conservatory has also earned notional recognition, winning outstanding school and outstanding teacher, and the Youth America Grand Prix competition multiple times, beyond technical training. The organization is committed to building confidence and resilience in its students, creating a supportive community where young dancers can thrive.
