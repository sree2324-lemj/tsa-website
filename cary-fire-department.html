Cary Fire Department

The Cary Fire Department provides fire protection, emergency medical response, and technical rescue, and many more services. The department serves a population of about 180,000 people and operates out of 9 different fire stations. The Cary Fire Department employs more than 240 capable firefighters and staff organized into many divisions.

The Cary Fire Department focuses on community risk reduction and safety education. meaning they prevent fires and save lives. The department includes a Fire Marshal Division that inspects and enforces fire codes to keep buildings safe. Recently, one of Cary's fire stations has become the first in the nation to earn a UL Verified Healthy Building Mark fr indoor air quality, showing just how much they care about firefighter health.
