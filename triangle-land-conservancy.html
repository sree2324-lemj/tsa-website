Triangle Land Conservancy

The Triangle Land Conservancy is a non-profit land conservation organization that works to protect the natural land of North Carolina. Since 1983, the organization has conserved over 23,000 acres of land across the region, working with private landowners who choose to protect their property. They are headquartered at 514 S. Duke Street in Durham, NC, and operate across 6 counties, including Wake. The Triangle Land Conservancy is very widely recognized for conservation excellence and ethical practices.

The Triangle Land Conservancy's mission is to safeguard clean water, protect natural habitats, support local farms and food, and connect people with nature. The organization also owns and manages 8 nature preserves that are open to the public. Beyond preserving land, the organization also works to make farmland more accessible so that more people have food to eat.
