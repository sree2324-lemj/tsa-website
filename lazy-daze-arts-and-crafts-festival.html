Lazy Daze Arts and Crafts Festival

The Lazy Daze Arts and Crafts Festival is a beloved annual event hosted by the Town of Cary. The event has brought many performers, artists, and community members together for over 50 years. Held every 4th week of August at the Cary Town Hall, the festival features over 250 artists from across the United States that you can look at for free for 2 days. The Lazy Daze Arts and Crafts Festival also has its fair share of handmade crafts and unique creations that are sure to make people laugh. With its summer atmosphere and something for every age group, the festival has become one of the top-rated craft festivals in the country.

Beyond the art booths, the Lazy Daze Arts and Crafts Festival gives a rich and diverse experience for everyone, no matter who you are. There are many different people who perform in the festival, ranging from local talent to regional performers from different countries. The festival also includes interactive art activities like artisan demonstrations and eating food from over 25 different food vendors. All of this makes the festival extremely popular in North Carolina.
