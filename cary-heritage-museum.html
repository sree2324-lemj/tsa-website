Cary Heritage Museum

The Cary Heritage Museum is located in the Page-Walker Arts & History Center in downtown cary. The museum serves as a hub for local history and education. The museum has possession of artifacts, photographs, and documents of the early life od cary. It offers many interactive experiences and educational programs for students and alike to learn about the vast history of Cary. Visitors can learn about Cary's growth from a rural community into the modern town it is today.

The Museum holds school field trips, workshops, and lectures helping students understand and appreciate everything the City of Cary has gone through. Special exhibits and cool rotating displays show different parts of local history, art, and culture. The Museum also uses many different activities and interactions, which can help keep the museum fresh and relevant. By preserving important things that happen in our community, it helps maintain a sense of identity and continuity.
