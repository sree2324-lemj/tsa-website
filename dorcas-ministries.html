Dorcas Ministries

Dorcas Ministries is a nonprofit organization based in Cary that has been serving the community since 1968. Its core mission is to provide compassionate assistance to local areas, helping families and individual people move from dependency to stability. The organization is located at the Dorcas Plaza at 187 High House Rd in Cary. In 2008, Dorcas Ministries bought a shopping plaza known as the Dorcas Plaza, which helps the organization achieve its mission.

Dorcas Ministries provides many services to those in need. These include crisis financial help, emergency food assistance, job training, and access to community resources. Its massive food pantry allows shoppers to hand-pick a variety of nutritious options, from canned goods to frozen meat and produce, ensuring that people are getting the vitamins that they need and being treated with dignity and respect. The organization also runs a thrift shop that generates its own revenue, which is then used to fund client services and programs. Recently, Dorcas even partnered with Advance Community Health to bring affordable healthcare directly to the Cary community.
